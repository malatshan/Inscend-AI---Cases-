THE CHALLENGE
The Gap Between AI Ambition
and Operational Reality
A multi-billion dollar wealth management firm recognized the strategic importance of AI. The challenge was not awareness. It was the absence of a structured path from intent to execution.
01
No unified AI strategy. No framework to evaluate feasibility, quantify value, or sequence implementation across the enterprise.
02
High cost of getting it wrong. In regulated wealth management, a poorly scoped AI initiative erodes confidence across compliance, leadership, and client relationships.
03
No infrastructure for AI oversight. No governance layer to monitor performance, manage risk, or maintain audit readiness at scale.
THE ENGAGEMENT
Inscend was engaged to architect the full journey: identify where AI creates measurable value, produce implementation-ready specifications, and build the governance platform to manage it all.
